Illuminations & tiny letters

Old manuscripts whisper of gilded pages, careful scribes, and tiny painted apples hidden at margins. I sketch and collect reproductions and love the little surprises in illuminated borders.

  • Gilded Initials — such patience, such sparkle.
  • Herbal Margins — tiny plants and curious creatures peeking out.
  • Songbooks — the music seems to float from the page.
